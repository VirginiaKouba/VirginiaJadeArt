Personalized Pet Portraits

Looking for a keepsake of your furry family member? I would be honored to create that for you! I work with clients to create personalized digital or colored pencil portraits.

Please contact me if you would like a portrait. I offer two portrait sizes in colored pencil. I am open to doing almost any pose you would like, e.g. full body, head and shoulders. I also offer the option of adding a background at no additional charge.
